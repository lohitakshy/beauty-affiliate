Our Story

Luxury beauty, curated with love

Welcome to Nacré

Nacré — the French word for mother-of-pearl — was born from a simple belief: that finding the right beauty product should feel as luxurious as using it. We are an independent beauty editorial platform dedicated to curating the finest skincare, makeup, fragrance, and haircare finds from the world's most trusted retailers.

Every product featured on nacre.beauty has been researched, reviewed, and selected with care. We compare prices across Sephora, Amazon, Glossier, Ulta, and more — so you always find the best option for your needs and budget.

Our editorial process

Our team researches trending and best-selling products across Sephora, Amazon, and leading beauty publications. We analyse thousands of customer reviews, ingredient lists, and expert recommendations to bring you only the most worthy finds.

We are proud to be an approved affiliate partner of Amazon Associates and Sephora via Rakuten Advertising. When you shop through our links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep nacre.beauty free and independent.
