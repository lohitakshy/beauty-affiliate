Our take

Baccarat Rouge 540 by Maison Francis Kurkdjian is a luminous, unforgettable fragrance that blends jasmine, saffron, and cedarwood into a warm, amber-floral signature that lingers for hours. Its iconic skin-like warmth and sillage make it a true luxury statement that turns heads and sparks conversations wherever you go.

How to use

Spritz 2-3 times onto pulse points — wrists, neck, and behind the ears — for maximum diffusion and longevity. Layer over an unscented moisturizer to lock in the scent and make it last even longer throughout the day.
