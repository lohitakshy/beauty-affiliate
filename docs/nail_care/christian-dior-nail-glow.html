Our take

Dior Nail Glow is a sheer, rosy enhancer that instantly makes bare nails look healthier, pinker, and perfectly polished without heavy color. Its lightweight formula doubles as a treatment, strengthening nails while delivering that effortless French-manicure effect in one swipe.

How to use

Start with clean, bare nails and apply one coat of Dior Nail Glow from base to tip, allowing 60 seconds to dry. For a more intense rosy effect, layer a second coat and finish with a clear top coat to extend wear.
